Team Section

A three-column team section with photos, roles and social links.

How to import

  1. Install and activate Free Widgets For Elementor (and Elementor) on your site.
  2. Go to Free Widgets → Demo Library in wp-admin.
  3. Find Team Section and click Import — the section is added to Templates → Saved Templates and its images to your Media Library.
  4. Edit any page with Elementor and insert the saved template wherever you like.
