Documentation Article

A documentation-style article with an auto-built, clickable table of contents that follows the reader down the page.

How to import

  1. Install and activate Free Widgets For Elementor (and Elementor) on your site.
  2. Go to Free Widgets → Demo Library in wp-admin.
  3. Find Documentation Article and click Import — the section is added to Templates → Saved Templates and its images to your Media Library.
  4. Edit any page with Elementor and insert the saved template wherever you like.
