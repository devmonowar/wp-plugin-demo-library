Countdown Banner

A bold launch banner with an animated countdown timer and a call-to-action button.

How to import

  1. Install and activate Free Widgets For Elementor (and Elementor) on your site.
  2. Go to Free Widgets → Demo Library in wp-admin.
  3. Find Countdown Banner and click Import — the section is added to Templates → Saved Templates and its images to your Media Library.
  4. Edit any page with Elementor and insert the saved template wherever you like.
