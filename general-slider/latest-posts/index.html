Latest Posts

A dynamic slider that builds itself from your newest blog posts — featured image, title, excerpt and link — and refreshes as you publish. Fade-up text, Ken Burns zoom.

How to import

  1. Install and activate General Slider on your WordPress site.
  2. Go to General Slider → Demo Library in wp-admin.
  3. Find Latest Posts and click Import Demo — the slider and its images are added automatically.
