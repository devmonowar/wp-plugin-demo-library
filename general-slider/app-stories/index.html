App Stories

A mobile-app hero slider wearing the Stories Progress skin — the current segment fills over the autoplay interval, just like app stories.

How to import

  1. Install and activate General Slider on your WordPress site.
  2. Go to General Slider → Demo Library in wp-admin.
  3. Find App Stories and click Import Demo — the slider and its images are added automatically.
