Minimal Clean

An understated, text-focused slider that lets your message lead. Wears the Minimal skin (thin arrows, line pagination).

How to import

  1. Install and activate General Slider on your WordPress site.
  2. Go to General Slider → Demo Library in wp-admin.
  3. Find Minimal Clean and click Import Demo — the slider and its images are added automatically.
