Product Carousel

A multi-slide carousel for products, categories and offers — shows several slides at once.

How to import

  1. Install and activate General Slider on your WordPress site.
  2. Go to General Slider → Demo Library in wp-admin.
  3. Find Product Carousel and click Import Demo — the slider and its images are added automatically.
