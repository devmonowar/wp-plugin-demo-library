Restaurant

A warm, appetising hero slider for restaurants, cafes and food businesses — with thumbnail navigation for browsing the dishes.

How to import

  1. Install and activate General Slider on your WordPress site.
  2. Go to General Slider → Demo Library in wp-admin.
  3. Find Restaurant and click Import Demo — the slider and its images are added automatically.
