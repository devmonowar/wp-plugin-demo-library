Creative Agency

A bold split-layout slider for studios and agencies — visuals on one side, message on the other.

How to import

  1. Install and activate General Slider on your WordPress site.
  2. Go to General Slider → Demo Library in wp-admin.
  3. Find Creative Agency and click Import Demo — the slider and its images are added automatically.
