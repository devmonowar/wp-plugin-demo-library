Nature Showcase

A cinematic full-width hero slider with a gradient overlay, Ken Burns zoom and animated text.

How to import

  1. Install and activate General Slider on your WordPress site.
  2. Go to General Slider → Demo Library in wp-admin.
  3. Find Nature Showcase and click Import Demo — the slider and its images are added automatically.
