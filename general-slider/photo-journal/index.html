A magazine-style photo story wearing the Numbers skin — 01 / 02 / 03 pagination with slow Ken Burns zoom over painterly landscapes.

How to import

  1. Install and activate General Slider on your WordPress site.
  2. Go to General Slider → Demo Library in wp-admin.
  3. Find Photo Journal and click Import Demo — the slider and its images are added automatically.
