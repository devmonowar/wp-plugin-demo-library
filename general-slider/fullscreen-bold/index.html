Fullscreen Bold

A dramatic full-height slider with bold typography for portfolios and landing pages.

How to import

  1. Install and activate General Slider on your WordPress site.
  2. Go to General Slider → Demo Library in wp-admin.
  3. Find Fullscreen Bold and click Import Demo — the slider and its images are added automatically.
